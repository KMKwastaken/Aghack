Steps to qualify for carbon markets

  1. 1 Confirm eligible land and baseline practices with a program.
  2. 2 Keep records of your management practices (dates, methods, acreage).
  3. 3 Maintain the practice over the required period (often 5–10 years).
  4. 4 Work with a carbon program or registry to enroll and report.
  5. 5 Complete verification or audit when required.
  6. 6 Receive issued credits and sell them on the market.

How it works

We multiply your farm size by a factor for your chosen practice to get an estimated amount of carbon sequestered or avoided each year (in tonnes of CO₂ equivalent). That number is then multiplied by a carbon price to give you a rough idea of potential annual revenue. Real programs use verified methodologies and may require soil testing, third-party audits, and adherence to specific timelines.
